Chronic Coder Academy

Aside from working on our own projects, we currently advise and assist 5 individuals who are learning ML by applying in their own ways to different online games. We call them the Chronic Coder Academy (CCA) Fellows!

Our trusty advisor (Ignacio Raposo - COO of Lugus Intelligence) and I personally call each of them on a weekly basis to discuss their new or ongoing ML projects - setting weekly todos, discussing potential resources and establishing next steps.

They are working on machine learning products that tackle problems in many other areas like League Of Legends, Valorant, Minecraft, and even Game Development!

More updates to come on their progress and projects soon. If you're impatient, however, please join the Discord to see their short progress updates on the CCA specific text channels and absolutely feel free to ask them about it!
