Chronic Coder Academy

Aside from working on our own projects, we run a fellowship that brings together groups of ML/Backend Engineers, Frontend Developers, and UX/UI Designers to build something impactful; and learn in that process. The program is called the Chronic Coder Academy (CCA) and each participant, once brought on, is a CCA Fellow!

The goal is to learn by building and shipping an application using the same technologies and best practices as leaders in the field. In addition to providing experience with specific tools, the platform serves as a portfolio piece to demonstrate that Fellows can successfully transition into backend/data science/frontend/user experience roles. Rather than just listing skills on a resume, Fellows can provide a concrete example of their experience that reduces the risk of hiring for teams.

To do this, we have meetings with each teams every week to go over progress, obstacles and action items for the week ahead. Furthermore, we have a fixed deadline of 9 weeks to go from ideation to production to testing. Hence, no time is wasted on features or aspects of each project that isn't essential. So far, we've had the opportunity to work with a diverse and passionate set of fellows from over 10 countries and 9 different timezones; the list continues to grow!
